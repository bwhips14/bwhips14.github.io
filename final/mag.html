Cupcheck

print & web magazine

CUPCHECK was a school project to design a magazine that supports the sciences and humanities. As per the faux letter from the editor: "We are CUPCHECK magzine...we know you work, play, fight, self-medicate, and repeat. We know you're hyped about sports, and about life. And probably most importantly, we know you are not an idiot."

The idea behind CUPCHECK is that a sports magazine can be fun, edgy, and also present intelligent articles that will provide athletes, coaches, and trainers with the necessary information to do what they do at the highest level possible.

my role

page layout, text design, text editing, illustrations, photo editing
