HIV/AIDS & the Economy of Africa

print booklet

Full Title: "Ten Things to Know about HIV/AIDS and the Economy of Africa;" this booklet presents a critical analysis of ten topics dealing with the AIDS epidemic in Africa. It is intended for those already educated in the field of economics, but written in a way that even those who are less educated in the field can understand. The ten topics include: "South Africa's Unemployment Rate," "Premature Mortality Expendatures," "The TRIPS Agreement," and "AIDS R & D Costs" to name a few.

The intent of the presentation of this booklet is to convince individuals of the importance of supporting AIDS research and providing medication and information to the people of African nations. It is my belief that only by helping developing nations early on can they become self-sufficient participants in global trade. Heavily based around statistical data, the booklet contains several information graphics to explain the data in the simplest possible way.

my role

research, page layout, text design, text editing, vector illustrations, infographic design
