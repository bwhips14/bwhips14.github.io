Away by Tyler Aucoin

album art

The album Away by Tyler Aucoin takes a step away from his heavier influences to present a steady and consistent serenade of brilliant acoustic guitar instrumentals. The masterful picking, plucking, and strumming sends the listener through a blend of classical and modern guitar riffs. This is my original concept art for the design of the CD packaging. It keeps with the steady, roaming atmosphere of the album and of its title, as well as its classic feel.

Note: I do not own any of the music. All rights reserved to artist Tyler Aucoin and producer Chris Piquette of Boundaries Studios, Providence, RI (2014). Away is available now on iTunes.

E Motion was a theoretical brand identity project. E Motion is a ficticious electric car rental company whose target audience is environmentally conscious young adults and parents concerned about the future. Competing with other rental companies such as Hertz, Thrifty, and National, E Motion is the only company to rent out electric cars exclusively.

In keeping with the customer's expectations of competing companies brands, the identity of E Motion is meant to be bold, professional, and above all friendly. In a time when most modern corperations are impersonal and centered around economic metrics, this brand harkens back to a time of friendly neighborhood businesses. Being the center of the brand, its logo stands clean, legible, and emotive, with a nostalgic feel for neon signs.
