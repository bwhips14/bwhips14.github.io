Boston in Black & White

photography

After living in a particular environment for a while, you can become complacent with the familiar sights of your surroundings. Boston in Black & White is my attempt to bring some of the spark back to one of America's oldest cities. More importantly, it was an opportunity for me to personally go out and look at the city with new eyes and just enjoy what it has to offer, rather than letting it pass me by.

As a set, Boston in Black & White represents the very essence that is the city of Boston. It is the people and communities, the neighborhood stomping grounds, and the natural beauty that make up this amazing city. Captured during the summer of 2014, these photos are part of a larger body of work and depict some of the most culturally and personally significant places in Boston.
