First Things First

stop motion animation

Based on the 'First Things First Manifesto' as laid out by a group of professional designers, this video speaks out for reorganizing our priorities away from advertising and toward moral and ethic public service work. It does not ask for an abolishment of advertising or capitalism as a whole, but a more simple belief that when man takes care of his fellow man first, then everyone is better prepared to live in a democratic society.

The video is a stop-motion animation comprised of over 300 still photos, and traces the theme of change through the metasymbol of the butterfly.

my role

concept, video direction, editing, and soundtrack, butterfly origami
